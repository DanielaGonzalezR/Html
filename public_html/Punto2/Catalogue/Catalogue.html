Sandwich

The bread is toasted. Wash and slice the tomatoes.Cooking on the grilled salted beef fillets.Preparar two French omelettes with the four eggs. Divide each tortilla into two parts. Assemble the sandwiches: a slice of toast, two slices of ham, two slices of cheese, two slices of sliced tomato breaded on mayonnaise on both sides, lettuce, breast fillets, a piece of tortilla. To finish the sandwich, spread a slice of bread with mayonnaise on one side and place on top. Divide the sandwiches diagonally and serve.

Chopped

To marinate the ribs, the minced meat in pieces and the wings or colombines. Cook over low heat with a little oil. In a separate frying pan fry the cassava, French fries and Creole potatoes in hot oil. In another put to cook with water and then saute the sausages in their own oil. If you have a grill you can put all the above with the ripe banana and roast it to keep it warm for serving time. The tomatoes are cut in julienne to accompany this dish with lemon. Served with guacamole to taste.

Pork Shop

The pork is cut into two-centimeter dice. And inserted into the spikes. Put four tablespoons of soy sauce in a fountain, add the same amount of water and garlic cloves peeled and chopped. Set the meat skewers and leave for two hours, turning them once in a while. After that time, grease the plate lightly, heat it over a hot fire and cook the skewers for about 15 minutes, turning them and watering them from time to time with the liquid of the marinade. They are passed to a fountain and are brought to the table fast.

Chopped

To marinate the ribs, the minced meat in pieces and the wings or colombines. Cook over low heat with a little oil. In a separate frying pan fry the cassava, French fries and Creole potatoes in hot oil. In another put to cook with water and then saute the sausages in their own oil. If you have a grill you can put all the above with the ripe banana and roast it to keep it warm for serving time. The tomatoes are cut in julienne to accompany this dish with lemon. Served with guacamole to taste.
